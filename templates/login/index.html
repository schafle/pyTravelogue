Travelogue

Travelogue is an application where one can log h[is/er] train journeys, maintain details of each journey and see beautiful statistics of them with timely stats, map displays and total distance travelled.

How to Use?

Enter your journey details

You can fill the details of the journeys you have travelled. You can also use pnr of your future journeys to fetch details automatically.

Maintain your profile

Keep a personal profile with map and statistics about your journeys. You can write detailed experience of your journey so that you can cherish your memorable moments.

Share your journeys

You can keep your data protected or share it with your friends.
